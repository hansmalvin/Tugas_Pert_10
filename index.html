The natural bioenergy remains intimately linked to both nutritious food and a balanced healthy lifestyle. The global epidemics of overweight- obesity and associated diseases such as type 2 diabetes, heart disease, high blood pressure, metabolic syndrome, hypothyroidism, cancer, and many more are the direct results of unhealthy foods, unbalanced lifestyle, and physical inactivity.
